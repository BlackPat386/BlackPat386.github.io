Daytona Beach OceanWalk

Restaurants and attractions

Food and Drinks

Daytona Beach OceanWalk is home to a variety of Restaurants of many different cuisines from pizza to sushi tacos. but what really brings tourists flocking to this location is the abundance of seafood dishes. Inspired by tropical cuisines, Restaurants such as Bubba Gump shrimp Co. and Joe's crab shack are hotspots for its great food and accessibility to the beach.


An addition to the many varieties of food, Daytona's OceanWalk hosts multiple beachside cabanas such as the the Ocean deck beach club, Waves beach bar at the Hilton resort, and the Oasis tiki bar. Many of these bars are open fairly early during peaks seasons of the the year from 9am to 11PM.

Boardwalk

Besides eating and drinking, Daytona beach's Boardwalk has a variety of ocean-side attractions and family operated shops. All year round you can expect tourists from all over to flock to the many arcades, gift shops and snackbars. Aside from that visitors are also treated to live music and performers throught the day.


What also makes the Boardwalk a great destination when visiting daytona beach is it's accessibility to the beach. located just a few meters from the ocean, the boardwalk allows visitors and beach goers to have a full day of fun on the oceanside and shopping without the need for transportation. In addition to the plethora of attractions, the boardwalk also is home to several rollercoasters and mini-kart races.
