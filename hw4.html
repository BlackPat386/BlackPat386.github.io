University of Central Florida

The university of central florida is a public research university that resides in Orlando,Florida with multiple smaller campuses throught the central florida region. With over 68,000 students enrolled in 2022, UCF has the second-largest student body in the United States next to Texas A&M university. Found and opened in 1963 and 1968, UCF has played a critical role in supporting space operations at the Kennedy Space Center on Florida's space coast.
