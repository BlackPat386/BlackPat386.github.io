Freecodecamp Sacramento


Freecodecamp Sacramento is a web development online community that delves into varoius topics such as HTML, CSS , Javascript, and Python etc. The groups goes in-depth on any given WebDev topic of the week and shows walkthroughs to accomplish certain tasks. Multiple attendees are able to collaborate and and give real-time feedback on these discussions



The group meets every two weeks on Saturdays via zoom to discuss and collaborate on the topic of the week. This week in particular was Java, and while i don't have much experience in that area it was interesting to get a brief taste of programming languages. The meeting was very informative while the host was able to effectively broke down various Java concepts.
